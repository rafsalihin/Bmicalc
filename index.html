About BMI

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.
Instructions:
  • Enter your weight and height according to correct unit.
  • For metric use centimeter(cm) and kilogram(kg).
  • For imperial use inches(in) and pounds(lbs).
  • Select "Calculate" and your BMI will appear below.
BMI Categories BMI Range
Underweight < 18.5
Normal weight 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight 25 - 29.9
Obesity >= 30
